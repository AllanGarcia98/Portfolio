My Services

I offer a wide range of makeup and hairstyle services, each customized to meet the unique needs and preferences of my clients. Whether you are looking for a natural, everyday look or a glamorous, bold style, I am here to help you achieve your desired look.

Makeup Services

  • Bridal Makeup: Elegant and timeless makeup for your special day, ensuring you look stunning in every photo.
  • Flower Girl Makeup: Soft and delicate makeup for the youngest members of your bridal party.
  • Special Occasion Makeup: Perfect for proms, parties, and other special events where you want to look your best.
  • Bridal Preview: A trial session to design and perfect the makeup look for your wedding day.

Hairstyle Services

  • Bridal Hairstyle: Stunning bridal hairstyles tailored to complement your wedding dress and overall look.
  • Updos: Elegant updos for formal events, providing a polished and sophisticated appearance.
  • Semi Updos: A versatile style that combines elements of updos and loose hair for a chic look.
  • Waves: Beautiful waves that add volume and texture to your hair, perfect for any occasion.
